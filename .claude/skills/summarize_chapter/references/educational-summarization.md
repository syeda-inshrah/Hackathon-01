# Educational Summarization Guidelines

## Objective
Create concise and accurate summaries suitable for learning and revision.

## Principles
- Preserve original meaning and structure
- Focus on key learning outcomes
- Avoid introducing new concepts

## Style
- Clear and academic
- Bullet points preferred
- No excessive simplification

## Use Cases
- Chapter overviews
- Quick revision material
- Learning reinforcement

## Educational Value
Well-structured summaries enhance comprehension and retention,
especially in technical subjects like robotics and AI.
