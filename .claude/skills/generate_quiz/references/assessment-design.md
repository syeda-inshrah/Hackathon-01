# Assessment Design Guidelines

## Objective
Generate meaningful multiple-choice questions that assess understanding.

## Principles
- Questions must be derived strictly from chapter content
- Avoid trivial or purely memorization-based questions
- Test conceptual understanding

## Question Design
- One correct answer
- Three plausible distractors
- Clear and unambiguous wording

## Educational Purpose
Quizzes help reinforce learning and allow students to self-evaluate
their understanding of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics concepts.

## Alignment
Supports course assessments and learning outcomes defined in the textbook.
